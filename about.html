About Metamo Surface Studio

Crafting Unforgettable Surfaces, Inspiring Spaces.

Our Vision

To be the leading innovator in textured paint solutions, transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences through sustainable, high-quality, and artistically diverse surface finishes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower designers, architects, and homeowners with versatile and durable textured paint products. We commit to:

  • Providing premium, eco-friendly materials.
  • Fostering creativity through a wide range of finishes and colors.
  • Delivering exceptional customer support and collaboration.
  • Driving innovation in surface technology.

Our Values

At Metamo Surface Studio, our core values guide every brushstroke and decision:

  • Innovation: Constantly exploring new techniques and materials.
  • Quality: Unwavering commitment to superior products and finishes.
  • Sustainability: Dedicated to environmentally responsible practices.
  • Collaboration: Building strong partnerships with clients and industry professionals.
  • Integrity: Operating with transparency, honesty, and respect.

Why Choose Metamo Surface Studio?

With years of expertise in the textured paint industry, we offer:

  • A diverse portfolio of unique finishes (Vellma, Vellplas, Vellcco, Vellwax).
  • Customizable solutions to match any design aesthetic.
  • Products renowned for their durability and ease of application.
  • A team of specialists ready to provide technical support and artistic guidance.
