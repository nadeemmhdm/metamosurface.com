About MacoAvell Textured Paint Supplier

MacoAvell is a Malaysian brand of textured paint supplier and manufacturer, specializing in premium lime-based textured paint, Venetian plaster, and wax finishes for interior and exterior textured walls. MacoAvell is available in a range of stunning colours, the range of Eco Friendly Textured Wall effects, finishes, and wall designs that are almost limitless. Here are the main product lines: Vellma, Vellplas, Vellcco, Vellwax, and Vellwax Metallic. Available in Glossy, Matte, Rough, Stones, and metallic. Learn more from our YouTube channel.
