About Metamo Textured Paint Supplier

At Metamo Surface Studio, we bring walls to life with premium texture paint solutions designed for beauty, durability, and style. Whether you’re building something new or refreshing an existing space, our high-quality textured finishes offer a distinctive touch that lasts.


From subtle patterns to bold designs, we help homeowners, designers, and builders add personality and depth to every surface. Our products are formulated to withstand time, weather, and wear — all while delivering a stunning visual impact.
